About BrightSmile

A clinic built on trust & expertise

Founded in 2008 in Mumbai, BrightSmile has grown from a two-chair practice into a full-service dental centre with 12 specialists. We believe every patient deserves personalised, compassionate care.

State-of-the-art facility — Digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, CAD/CAM systems, and laser dentistry for precision treatment.

Patient-first philosophy — We listen, explain clearly, and ensure you're comfortable at every stage.

Certified professionals — All doctors hold MDS degrees and attend regular continuing education.

Frequently Asked

Do you accept walk-in patients?+
Yes, we welcome walk-ins, though we recommend booking in advance to avoid waiting. Use our form below to secure a confirmed slot.
Is Invisalign painful?+
Most patients experience mild pressure during the first few days of each new tray — this is normal and indicates your teeth are moving. No sharp pain is expected.
How long does teeth whitening last?+
Professional whitening typically lasts 1–3 years depending on your diet and oral hygiene habits. We offer affordable touch-up sessions for existing patients.
Do you offer EMI options?+
Yes! We offer 0% interest EMI on all treatments above ₹10,000 via major credit cards and Bajaj Finserv. Ask our front desk for details.
What are your clinic hours?+
We're open Mon–Sat 9am–8pm and Sunday 10am–2pm. Emergency dental care is available on call 24/7 at +91 98200 34567.
