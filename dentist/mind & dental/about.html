Our Mission

"Dentistry is not just about teeth. It is about confidence, connection, and the courage to smile without hesitation."

At BrightSmile, we built something Mumbai had never quite seen — a clinic where clinical excellence and genuine human warmth coexist. Where walking through our doors feels less like a medical appointment and more like a conversation with someone who truly cares.

Our founders, Dr. Ananya Sharma and Dr. Vikram Kumar, started with one vision: that every patient deserves to feel seen, heard, and cared for — regardless of what brought them to us.

Our Journey

Seventeen years of milestones

2008
The Beginning

A two-chair dream in Bandra

Dr. Ananya Sharma opens BrightSmile in a modest 400 sq. ft. space on Linking Road. With two dental chairs, one receptionist, and an unwavering belief that Mumbai deserved better dental care, the journey begins.

✦ First 100 patients within 3 months
2011
Expansion

First full-floor clinic opens

Demand outgrows the original space. BrightSmile relocates to a dedicated full-floor facility — 2,800 sq. ft. with eight treatment rooms, a dedicated X-ray suite, and a patient lounge that rethinks what waiting feels like.

✦ 3,000+ patients served
2013
Technology

Mumbai's first digital impression system

We become one of the first clinics in Mumbai to adopt full-arch digital scanning — eliminating uncomfortable impressions and enabling same-day temporary restorations. Patient satisfaction jumps to 97%.

✦ Best Innovation Award — Dental Forum India
2016
Certification

Certified Invisalign Platinum Provider

After treating over 500 orthodontic cases, BrightSmile earns Invisalign Platinum status — placing us in the top tier of providers across India. Dr. Rohan Mehta joins as lead orthodontist.

✦ 500+ clear aligner cases completed
2019
Recognition

Top 10 Dental Clinics in India

Dental Asia Magazine names BrightSmile among India's top 10 dental practices. The team grows to 12 doctors, a full implant suite launches, and laser dentistry becomes part of our standard toolkit.

✦ National Award — Dental Asia Magazine 2019
2022
Redesign

The boutique clinic concept is born

A full clinic redesign — internationally sourced dental chairs, ambient soundscaping, curated artwork, and bespoke patient experiences. BrightSmile stops being "just a dentist" and becomes a destination.

✦ 8,000+ lifetime patients milestone
2025
Today

Still growing, still caring

Seventeen years in, BrightSmile serves 10,000+ patients with 12 in-house specialists, 98% satisfaction, and a philosophy unchanged since day one: every person deserves the very best.
